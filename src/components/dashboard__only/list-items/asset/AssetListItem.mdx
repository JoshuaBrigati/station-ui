# AssetListItem

##### List item for the Asset page. does not contain background.

## Props
- **`currencySymbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the currency used in the list item.
<br />

- **`firstColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The first column of the list item.
- - **`symbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the token.
- - **`symbolIcon`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The icon of the token.
- - **`chainName`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The name of the chain.
- - **`chainIcon`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The icon of the chain.
<br />

- **`secondColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The second column of the list item.
- - **`currentPrice`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` - The current price of the token.
<br />

- **`thirdColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The third column of the list item.
- - **`percentage`** - <RequiredText /> - `boolean` - How much the token went up or down in the selected period.
<br />

- **`fourthColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The fourth column of the list item.
- - **`symbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the token.
- - **`currencyAmount`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` -  The amount of currency.
- - **`tokenAmount`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The amount of tokens.
<br />

## Import
```jsx
import { AssetListItem } from "@terra-money/station-ui"
```
