# StakeListItem

##### List item for the Stake page. does not contain background.

## Props
- **`variant`** - <RequiredText /> - `"default" | "staked" | "unstaked"` - Used to determine the styling of the list item.
- **`currencySymbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the currency used in the list item.
<br />

- **`firstColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The first column of the list item.
- - **`symbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the token.
- - **`symbolIcon`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The icon of the token.
- - **`chainName`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The name of the chain.
- - **`chainIcon`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The icon of the chain.
<br />

- **`secondColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The second column of the list item.
- - **`tokenAmount`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` - The amount of tokens.
- - **`tokenSymbol`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The symbol of the token.
- - **`currencyAmount`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` - The amount of currency.
<br />

- **`thirdColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The third column of the list item.
- - **`isAlliance`** - <RequiredText /> - `boolean` - Whether or not the token is an alliance token.
<br />

- **`fourthColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The fourth column of the list item.
- - **`variant`** - <RequiredText /> - `"default" | "staked" | "unstaking"` - Used to determine the styling of the fourth column.
- - **`defaultUnstakeDays`** - `number` - The default amount of days to unstake.
- - **`claimableCurrencyAmount`** - `number` - The amount of currency that can be claimed.
- - **`unstakingTimer`** - `string` - The amount of time until unstaking is complete.
<br />

- **`fifthColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The fifth column of the list item.
- - **`variant`** - <RequiredText /> - `"default" | "staked" | "unstaked"` - Used to determine the styling of the fifth column.
- - **`defaultAPR`** - `number` - The default APR.
- - **`stakedDetails`** - `object` - The details of the staked token.
    - **`validatorName`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The name of the validator.
    - **`validatorCommission`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` - The commission of the validator.
- - **`unstakedDetails`** - `object` - The details of the unstaked token.
    - **`validatorName`** - <RequiredText /> - `string` - The name of the validator.
    - **`validatorCommission`** - <RequiredText /> - `number` - The commission of the validator.
<br />
- **`sixthColumn`** - <RequiredText /> - `object` - The sixth column of the list item.
- - **`variant`** - <RequiredText /> - `"default" | "staked" | "unstaked"` - Used to determine the styling of the sixth column.
- - **`manageOnClick`** - `function` - The function to call when the manage button is clicked.
- - **`newStakeOnClick`** - `function` - The function to call when the new stake button is clicked.
- - **`buttonDisabled`** - `boolean` - Whether or not the button is disabled.

## Import
```jsx
import { StakeListItem } from "@terra-money/station-ui"
```
